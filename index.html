Our Mission

Connecting Young Professionals in the Amherst area.

Our Goals

To help young professionals build both personal and professional relationships with one another.

To support community development making the Amherst area a more attractive place for young professionals, businesses and entrepreneurs.

To build community through raising awareness, working with community organizations and fundraising.

To have fun.

Member Directory

What does a $30 annual membership to YPA provide you?

  • You will be able to connect with other young professionals
  • There will be organized afterhours networking events to help broaden both professional and personal relationships
  • There will be organized unique events that highlight the area and all it has to offer
  • You will be able to make a positive impact in the community through increasing business awareness in the area and help strengthen the local economy
  • By being a member, you will be able to participate in promotions offered by various restaurants, organizations, local shops and entertainment offered exclusively to YPA members
